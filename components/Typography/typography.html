Spacing Utilities

Memorable-UI includes a wide range of shorthand responsive margin and padding utility classes to modify an element’s appearance. It comes with different flexible spacing utilities of different sizes, such as- extra-small, small, medium, large, extra-large,and extra-extra-large. The sizes are defined as -

  • xs for extra-small (0.8rem)
  • sm for small (1.6rem)
  • md for medium (2.4rem)
  • lg for large (3.2rem)
  • xl for extra-large (4rem)
  • lg for extra-extra large (6.4rem)
