Card

The Card component is used to convey information about some particular topic/item.

The Card Component comes with different container sizes

  • xs-card-width(20rem)
  • sm-card-width(25rem)
  • md-card-width(30rem)
  • lg-card-width(35rem)
  • xl-card-width(40rem)
  • xxl-card-width(45rem)
Cards With Badges

Ribbon badges are a great way to highlight something on your website, especially on Card UI designs. This simple pure CSS ribbon badge can by easily placed on the top left,top right,bottom left,bottom right, top full, bottom full, middle full corner of your designs.

Card with Badges comes with different colors

  • blue
  • pink
  • orange
  • red
  • green
